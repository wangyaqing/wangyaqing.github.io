## Course: Machine Intelligence 
  Qiuzhen College, Tsinghua University  

  2025 Spring, 2025 Fall, 2026 Spring  

  Course ID: 84760933-0 

  *Machine Intelligence* is a course offered at Qiuzhen College, Tsinghua University, focusing on core topics in **machine learning (ML)** and **artificial intelligence (AI)**. Designed for students with a mathematics background, it emphasizes conceptual clarity and practical intuition, with examples in each lecture to connect theory and application. Topics include ML foundations, deep learning, and applications in vision, language, math and science.


## Seminar: BIMSA Colloquium
  BIMSA

  2026-now

  Co-Organized with 
  Prof. Tadahisa Funaki, Prof. Kimyeong Lee, and Prof. Jinsong Wu

  The **[BIMSA Colloquium](https://bimsa.net/activity/Colloquium/)** will invite scholars from outside the institution to share their cutting-edge research findings and significant advances in different fields. The aim is to help researchers stay informed about the latest developments across different fields, broaden their academic horizons, and promote interdisciplinary communication. During the talks, speakers will strive to present their topics in an accessible manner so that audience members with diverse academic backgrounds can grasp some of the key ideas. Even if audience cannot fully understand all aspects of a presentation, gaining just 30% of the content may still provide valuable inspiration and even lead to new research directions. Therefore, we encourage all members to actively participate and contribute to an open, diverse, and intellectually stimulating academic environment. If you are interested in giving a talk, please feel free to contact me.
